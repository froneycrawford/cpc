Franklin & Marshall College

Overview

    Franklin & Marshall College will be hosting the 34th Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium Astronomers' Meeting on Saturday, April 5, 2014. We welcome contributions from students and faculty alike on topics in astronomy, astrophysics, and astronomy education. The conference will be hosted in the Bonchek Auditorium in the LSP Building on the F&M campus.

Registration

    You can register for the conference by going to our registration link and filling out the web form. There is no registration fee, and all students, postdocs and faculty are welcome to attend.

    The deadline for registration is Monday, March 17.

Logistical Details
  • Wireless network access will be available for attendees during the conference. Instructions on how to access the wireless network will be provided when you arrive on campus.
  • Lunch will be served. Be sure to let us know of any allergies etc. when filling out the registration form.
Directions and Contact Information
    From Route 30, take the Harrisburg Pike exit and head east on Harrisburg Pike (this is a left turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the east, and a right turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the west). Travel a little over a mile on Harrisburg Pike until you see the Iron Hill Brewery at a traffic light. At the traffic light make a right turn into Williamson Parking Lot. The LSP Building's front entrance directly abuts the parking lot and is on the right as you drive into the parking lot from the street.
