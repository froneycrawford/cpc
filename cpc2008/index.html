28th Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium
Astronomers' Meeting

Saturday, April 12, 2008
Franklin and Marshall College
Barshinger Life Sciences and Philosophy Building

Overview

    Franklin and Marshall College will be hosting the 28th Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium Astronomers' Meeting on Saturday, April 12, 2008. We welcome contributions from students and faculty alike on topics in astronomy, astrophysics, and astronomy education. The conference will be hosted in the newly constructed Barshinger Life Sciences and Philosophy Building on the F&M campus.

Directions and Contact Information
    From Route 30, take the Harrisburg Pike exit (this is a left turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the east, and a right turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the west). Travel a little over a mile on Harrisburg Pike until you pass under a pedestrian bridge. Immediately afterwards (at the traffic light), make a right turn into Williamson Parking Lot. The Barshinger Life Sceinces and Philosophy Building is on the right side of the parking lot as you drive in.
