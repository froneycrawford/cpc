Overview

Franklin & Marshall College will be hosting the 40th Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium Astronomers' Meeting on Saturday, April 4, 2020. We welcome contributions from students and faculty alike on topics in astronomy, astrophysics, and astronomy education. The conference will be hosted in the Adams Auditorium in Kaufman Hall in the F&M campus.

Registration

You can register for the conference by filling out the registration form.

The deadline for registration is Friday, March 20, 2020.

There is no registration fee, and all students, postdocs and faculty are welcome to attend. Requests to give presentations can be indicated on the registration form.


Logistical Details

Wireless network access will be available for attendees during the conference. Instructions on how to access the wireless network will be provided when you arrive on campus.

Lunch will be served. Be sure to let us know of any allergies etc. when filling out the registration form.


Directions

By Car: From Route 30, take the Harrisburg Pike exit and head east on Harrisburg Pike (this is a left turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the east, and a right turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the west). Travel a little over a mile on Harrisburg Pike until you see a Wendy's and turf soccer field on your left, both about 50 yards before a traffic light. Make a left at this traffic light into the parking lot. In the lot bear right, and park. After parking, keep walking along Harrisburg Pike in the same direction you drove (east) about 50 yards to the next traffic light, which is right at Iron Hill Brewery. Walk across Harrisburg Pike at this light and enter the Williamson Lot. At the far end of Williamson Lot is the Kaufman building. Here is a map showing the location of the entrance of Williamson Parking Lot on Harrisburg Pike.

By Train: There is an Amtrak station (Lancaster Amtrak) within walking distance to the campus. The Amtrak station code for Lancaster is LNC, and here is more information about the station.
