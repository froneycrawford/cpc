Important Conference Update

Wednesday, March 11, 2020

Due to the spread of the COVID-19 virus, and restrictions on our campus around travel and large gatherings, we have made the difficult decision to cancel the 40th Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium Astronomers' Meeting at F&M scheduled for Saturday, April 4, 2020.

We are sorry that attendees will miss out on the opportunity to present their research and/or hear about other research that is happening in the CPC consortium, but health concerns must be a priority in these uncertain times. Questions can be directed to Lynn Johnson at lynn.johnson@fandm.edu or 717-291-4136.

We hope to see you at next year's CPC conference.
