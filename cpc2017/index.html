Franklin & Marshall College

Overview

    Franklin & Marshall College will be hosting the 37th Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium Astronomers' Meeting on Saturday, April 15, 2017. We welcome contributions from students and faculty alike on topics in astronomy, astrophysics, and astronomy education. The conference will be hosted in the Adams Auditorium in Kaufman Hall in the F&M campus.
