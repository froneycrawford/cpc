31st Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium
Astronomers' Meeting

Saturday, April 9, 2011
9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.
Franklin & Marshall College
Stahr Auditorium, Stager Hall

Overview

    Franklin & Marshall College will be hosting the 31st Annual Central Pennsylvania Consortium Astronomers' Meeting on Saturday, April 9, 2011.We welcome contributions from students and faculty alike on topics in astronomy, astrophysics, and astronomy education. The conference will be hosted in the Stahr Auditorium in the Stager Hall on the F&M campus.

Registration

    You can register for the conference by going to our registration link and filling out the web form. There is no registration fee, and all students, postdocs and faculty are welcome to attend.

    The deadline for registration is Friday April 1.

Logistical Details
  • Wireless network access will be available for attendees during the conference. Instructions on how to access the wireless network will be provided when you arrive on campus.
  • Lunch will be served. Be sure to let us know of any allergies etc. when filling out the registration form.
Directions and Contact Information
    From Route 30, take the Harrisburg Pike exit and head east on Harrisburg Pike (this is a left turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the east, and a right turn from the off ramp if you are coming from the west). Travel a little over a mile on Harrisburg Pike until you see the Iron Hill Brewery at a traffic light. At the traffic light make a right turn into Williamson Parking Lot. To get to the Stager Hall which houses the Stahr Auditorium, walk beyond the far end of the parking lot to a large quad. Continue through the quad and bear slightly left as you walk along the sidewalk path. After leaving the quad, Stager Hall is on your left about 40 yards from the edge of the quad. It is a large glass and brick building.
